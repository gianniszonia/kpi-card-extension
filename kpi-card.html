Getting Started
1Drag a Date field onto the Date encoding tile on the Marks card. The date must be continuous (green pill). Supports hourly, daily, weekly, monthly, and yearly granularity (auto-detected).
2Drag your main Measure onto the Measure encoding tile.
3Optionally, add a dimension to Detail to automatically break the card into multiple cards, or add a target measure / extra fields to Detail for target tracking, segmentation, coloring and tooltip context.
4Click Format Extension on the Marks card to configure widgets, pages, and styling.

About

A flexible, all-in-one KPI card extension for Tableau. Combine headline metrics, comparisons, targets, and charts into a single configurable card with automatic date granularity detection, rich formatting, and flexible multi-widget layouts.

Key Features
8 widget types: KPI Summary, Period-to-Date, Bar Chart, Waterfall Chart, Line Chart, Radial Bar Chart, Funnel Chart, Divider Line
Auto-detects granularity: hourly, daily, weekly, monthly, yearly
Split cards by any dimension with per-card overrides
Color bars by dimension or positive/negative values
Multi-page carousel with vertical or horizontal widget stacking
Custom delta comparisons across any number of periods
Target progress tracking with customizable target lines
Full-period and last-period calculation modes
Fully customizable tooltips, reference lines, and formatting defaults

Tip: Your saved defaults are worksheet-based and will persist even after closing and reopening the workbook. To carry your defaults to another worksheet, duplicate the worksheet rather than adding the extension from scratch. Each worksheet maintains its own independent defaults.
