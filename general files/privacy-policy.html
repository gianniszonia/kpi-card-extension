InstaKPI does not collect, store, or transmit any user data.

This extension runs entirely within your Tableau environment. No personal information, usage data, analytics, or any other data is ever collected, stored, or sent to any external server or third party — at any time.

About This Extension

Stop spending hours formatting and creating the same calculations every time. InstaKPI turns a measure and a date field into a stunning, fully interactive KPI card in seconds — with zero manual setup. Just drag, drop, done. Supports 8 widget types: KPI Summary, YTD Scorecard, Bar Chart, Line Chart, Waterfall, Radial Bar, Funnel Chart, and Divider Line. Every element is fully customizable through a built-in formatting panel with support for multi-page layouts, dark and light themes, custom deltas, target tracking, and more.

InstaKPI runs entirely within your environment. It never transmits, stores, or has any access to your data. What you see on screen stays on screen.

InstaKPI is provided completely free of charge. There are no paid tiers, subscriptions, or hidden fees.

Data Collection & Privacy

InstaKPI is designed to operate within Tableau's sandboxed extension environment, with the only exception being the use of custom fonts from the local `fonts/` folder. It does not:

  · Collect any personal or identifying information
  · Store any user data locally or remotely
  · Transmit any data to external servers
  · Use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics tools
  · Access any data outside of the Tableau worksheet it is embedded in

All data displayed in InstaKPI comes directly from the Tableau worksheet it is connected to and is never read, copied, or processed outside of your Tableau environment.

Extension Settings

InstaKPI stores formatting preferences (such as colors, font choices, and layout options) using Tableau's built-in settings API. These settings are saved within your Tableau workbook only and are never transmitted outside of Tableau.

Third-Party Services

InstaKPI does not integrate with or rely on any third-party services, APIs, or external libraries loaded from remote sources. All assets are bundled locally within the extension.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated in the future, the updated version will be made available through the same channel as this document. Continued use of InstaKPI constitutes acceptance of any revised policy.
